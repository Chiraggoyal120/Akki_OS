# AKKI Brand DNA - Configuration

## 1. The Enemy (What we fight against)
The outdated, one-size-fits-all education system.
- **Keywords:** Factory coaching, Rote memorization, "Marks > Mastery", Expensive offline systems.
- **Narrative:** "The system failed you, you didn't fail the system."

## 2. The Hero (Who we serve)
The ambitious but overwhelmed Tier 2/3 student.
- **Traits:** Hardworking but inconsistent, lacks guidance, needs structure.
- **Desire:** A mentor, not just content. Mastery, not just marks.
- **Product Context:** Vidyaa AI (Personalized AI Tutor/Mentor).

## 3. Voice Profile (The Vibe)
**Role:** The Calm, Analytical Founder-Mentor.
- **✅ DO:** Clear, Intelligent, Empathetic, Analytical, "Founder-first".
- **❌ DON'T:** Fake "10X" motivation, Hustle culture aggression, Corporate EdTech PR, Generic fluff.

## 4. Content Pillars
1.  **System Critique:** Why coaching factories are broken.
2.  **The AI Solution:** How personalized AI fixes the "one-size-fits-all" problem.
3.  **Study Psychology:** Analytical tips on consistency and mastery.
4.  **Founder Journey:** Building Vidyaa AI to solve the Tier 2/3 gap.
