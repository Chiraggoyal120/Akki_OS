\## Keyword Patterns (What Works)



\### Pattern #1: Long-Tail Keywords

\- \*\*Discovery:\*\* 3-5 word keywords easier to rank

\- \*\*Example:\*\* "best crm for small business" vs "crm"

\- \*\*Success Rate:\*\* 80% page 1 in 6 months

\- \*\*Volume Trade-off:\*\* Lower search volume but higher intent

\- \*\*Strategy:\*\* Always find long-tail opportunities



\### Pattern #2: Comparison Keywords

\- \*\*Discovery:\*\* "X vs Y" keywords have high commercial intent

\- \*\*Example:\*\* "Salesforce vs HubSpot"

\- \*\*Search Volume:\*\* Medium (200-500/mo typically)

\- \*\*Competition:\*\* Medium (achievable)

\- \*\*Conversion:\*\* High (buyers comparing)

\- \*\*Our Success:\*\* 5/7 comparison posts ranked page 1



\### Pattern #3: "Alternative to X"

\- \*\*Discovery:\*\* People searching alternatives are unhappy

\- \*\*Example:\*\* "alternative to Mailchimp"

\- \*\*Intent:\*\* Ready to switch (high commercial intent)

\- \*\*Competition:\*\* Surprisingly low

\- \*\*Our Success:\*\* 3/3 alternative posts ranked top 5



\## Search Intent Mastery



\### Intent Type Performance



\*\*Informational\*\* ("what is X", "how to Y")

\- Difficulty: Low

\- Traffic: High

\- Conversion: Low

\- Best for: Top-of-funnel, brand awareness



\*\*Commercial\*\* ("best X", "X vs Y", "X review")

\- Difficulty: Medium

\- Traffic: Medium

\- Conversion: High

\- Best for: Mid-funnel, consideration



\*\*Transactional\*\* ("X pricing", "buy X", "X trial")

\- Difficulty: High

\- Traffic: Low

\- Conversion: Very High

\- Best for: Bottom-funnel, conversion
